About the product and its builder

TFMS was not designed in a meeting room.

It was built from over 15 years of hands-on software development inside one of Southern Africa's largest tobacco leaf companies — in both Malawi and Mozambique.

The builder

Tafika Bilson Itaye Jr.

Photo placeholder

Tafika Bilson Itaye Jr.

IT Manager · Software Developer · Founder, TechNexus

An experienced IT Manager and Project Manager with over 15 years of technical and managerial expertise in enterprise systems, database administration, and software development. Proven track record leading IT initiatives across multinational environments in Malawi and Mozambique.

Tafika designed and maintained agronomy and factory processing software at Universal Leaf Tobacco from 2008 to 2023, directly managing systems that handled over 10,000 grower records and supported 17 provincial offices and buying centres. TFMS is a direct product of that operational expertise.
