The Builder

Tafika Bilson
Itaye Jr.

An experienced IT Manager and Project Manager with over 15 years of technical and managerial expertise in enterprise systems, database administration, and software development. Proven track record leading IT initiatives across multinational environments in Malawi and Mozambique.

Tafika designed and maintained agronomy and factory processing software at Universal Leaf Tobacco from 2008 to 2023, directly managing systems that handled over 10,000 grower records and supported 17 provincial offices and buying centres.

TFMS is a direct product of that operational expertise — not a concept product, not a pilot. Every module was shaped by real season cycles, real factory floor constraints, and real extension officer workflows in Southern Africa.
